Welcome!

Welcome to my website! I am a passionate neuroscientist and science communicator. I believe that sharing knowledge about the brain can help us all lead happier, healthier lives. On this site, you'll find insights from my personal journey in neuroscience, as well as resources for exploring the fascinating world of brain science. Whether you're a student, a researcher, or just someone curious about the mind, I hope you'll find something of interest here. Let's explore the wonders of the brain together!
